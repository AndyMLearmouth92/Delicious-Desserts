Our Story

Delicious Desserts opened in December 2013 when Sarah Foster decided to take her lifelong hobby of baking to the next level by opening her own luxury coffee and dessert house.

Nearly 10 years after opening, Delicious Desserts has grown to be one of the most loved bakeries in Newcastle upon Tyne. One of the key factors of our success has been to build a strong reputation based on the quality of our lovingly handmade cakes, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional baking methods. From stunning whole cakes to our pancakes, donuts and refreshments, we believe we have the perfect range of treats to delight all of our loyal customers both old and new.

All of our staff are trained to the highest standard to ensure we keep up with the latest culinary trends, regularly updating our menu with new and exciting desserts.
